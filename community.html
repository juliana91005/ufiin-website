Who We Are

University Female Investors International Network

Founded by the Opportunity Fund Project (OFP), UFIIN is a multinational online community of undergraduate women pursuing careers in finance and investing.

Our mission is to unite university women in finance — proving that our greatest strength is in our numbers.

A Community Made for You

We are building a space for you

We had an idea — that university women studying for a career in finance and investing have a challenging road ahead. So we built a collaborative online space where women can:

  • Connect with like-minded women across campuses
  • Access mentorship, guidance, and peer support
  • Ask questions without judgment
  • Learn investing fundamentals in a supportive environment
  • Feel confident navigating the world of finance
  • Build a network that lasts beyond university
