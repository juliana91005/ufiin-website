About UFIIN

We exist because
no one should invest
alone

The University Female Investors International Network is a peer-driven community built for the next generation of women in finance.

Our Story

Founded on a
simple belief

UFIIN was founded on a simple belief: female student investors deserve a space where they can take risks, ask questions, and grow without judgment.

We are a peer-driven community where every voice matters — from the first-time investor to the seasoned portfolio builder. No gatekeeping. No judgment. Just growth.

"I truly believe we can all help uplift one another, that way we can stress that no one is alone on this journey."
— UFIIN Founder
Life at UFIIN

Real members,
real connections

Our Mission

Closing the gap in
women's financial leadership

Women are underrepresented in finance and investing — but that is changing. UFIIN exists to accelerate that change by connecting the next generation of female investors.

We believe that when women share knowledge, support each other's goals, and build professional networks together — the entire industry becomes better for it.

From portfolio strategy to interview prep to moral support during a market downturn — UFIIN is there for every step.

Global Community

40+ universities,
one community

Our members span 12+ countries and 40+ universities. Whether you're at a target school or a smaller institution, UFIIN connects you to a global cohort of driven women who share your passion for investing.

No experience required. No specific school required. Just a shared belief that women belong in finance.
